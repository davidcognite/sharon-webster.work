Booking Calendar Redesign

National Trust Holiday Cottage Booking Experience

Role: Senior UX Researcher
Duration: 3 months
Methods: Usability Testing, Conversion Optimization

Project Overview

Improved the National Trust holiday cottage booking experience through user research and iterative design, focusing on better communication of booking rules and clearer user guidance throughout the reservation process.

The Challenge

The existing booking calendar had a low completion rate of just 21%, with users abandoning their bookings due to unclear booking rules and confusing interface elements that hindered the reservation process.

Research Approach

  • Conducted usability testing sessions with potential guests
  • Analysed user behaviour and drop-off points in the booking funnel
  • Implemented iterative design improvements based on user feedback
  • Focused on clearer communication of booking policies and availability

Outcomes & Impact

Increased checkout completion rate from 21% to 34%, representing a significant improvement in booking success and revenue generation for National Trust's holiday cottage programme.
