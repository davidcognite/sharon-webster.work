Creating Effective Data-Driven Information Architecture

Financial Advisor Portal IA Redesign

Role: Senior UX Researcher
Duration: 4 months
Methods: Card Sorting, Tree Testing, IA Design

Project Overview

Redesigned the information architecture for a financial advisor portal using data-driven research methods, achieving a 46% improvement in content findability through iterative testing and user-centred design principles.

The Challenge

Financial advisors were struggling to find relevant information quickly within the existing portal structure. The challenge was to create an intuitive IA that matched users' mental models while accommodating complex financial content.

Research Approach

  • Conducted card sorting exercises with financial advisors
  • Performed tree testing to validate proposed IA structures
  • Implemented iterative testing cycles for continuous improvement
  • Applied user-centred design principles throughout the process

Outcomes & Impact

Achieved 46% improvement in findability scores, significantly reducing the time financial advisors needed to locate critical information and improving overall portal efficiency and user satisfaction.
