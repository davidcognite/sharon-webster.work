Informing Strategy Through User-Centric Insights

Mental Health Digital Strategy Research

Role: Senior UX Researcher
Duration: 6 months
Methods: Strategic Research, Workshop Facilitation

Project Overview

Led a comprehensive research programme to inform mental healthcare digital strategy, conducting 15 interviews and facilitating 16 workshops with over 200 service providers to identify opportunities for digital transformation in mental health services.

The Challenge

Mental health services needed strategic direction for digital transformation to better serve patients and providers. The challenge was to gather insights from a diverse stakeholder group and translate complex needs into actionable strategic recommendations.

Research Approach

  • Conducted 15 in-depth stakeholder interviews
  • Facilitated 16 collaborative workshops with service providers
  • Engaged with 200+ participants across various mental health disciplines
  • Synthesised insights into strategic frameworks

Outcomes & Impact

Delivered 77 actionable recommendations that shaped the organisation's digital strategy, providing clear direction for technology investments and service improvements in mental healthcare delivery.
