Designing Research for People with Disabilities

Accessibility in Travel Research

Role: Senior UX Researcher
Duration: 8 months
Methods: Inclusive Research, Accessibility Testing

Project Overview

Conducted comprehensive inclusive research with people with hearing loss and autism to identify travel barriers and accessibility challenges, implementing robust safeguarding practices throughout the research process.

The Challenge

Travel services needed to understand the specific barriers faced by people with disabilities. The challenge was to design inclusive research methods that would capture authentic experiences while ensuring participant safety and comfort.

Research Approach

  • Developed inclusive research protocols with safeguarding measures
  • Conducted interviews with people with hearing loss and autism
  • Implemented accessibility accommodations for all research activities
  • Created comprehensive analysis framework for accessibility barriers

Outcomes & Impact

Generated 128 actionable insights for accessibility improvements across travel services, providing clear guidance for making travel more inclusive and accessible for people with disabilities.
